Traditions

Guddi Padwa

Gudi Padwa marks the traditional New Year for Maharashtrians, symbolizing prosperity and new beginnings.

Ganpati Festival

Ganpati Festival is a joyful celebration of Lord Ganesha’s arrival and blessings.

Vada Pav

Vada Pav is a popular Mumbai street food consisting of a spicy potato fritter sandwiched in a bun..

Moddak

Modak is a sweet dumpling, typically filled with coconut and jaggery, and is a favorite dish of Lord Ganesha.

Location

Maharashtra is a state located in the western part of India, occupying a substantial portion of the Deccan Plateau.

Shivaji Maharaj

Shivaji Maharaj was a valiant Maratha king known for his bravery, leadership, and founding the Maratha Empire.
