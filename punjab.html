Traditions

Guru Nanak Jayanti

It celebrates the birth anniversary of Guru Nanak Dev Ji, the first Sikh Guru and the founder of Sikhism.

Baisakhi

It marks the harvest season in Punjab and holds religious significance for Sikhs as the day Guru Gobind Singh Ji established the Khalsa Panth in 1699.

Butter Chicken

A creamy, spiced chicken curry.

Aloo Paratha

Flatbread stuffed with spiced mashed potatoes.

Location

Punjab is in northern India, bordered by Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, and Pakistan.

Lohri

Lohri marks the end of winter and the beginning of the harvest season, particularly for crops like sugarcane and wheat.
