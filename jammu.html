Traditions

Hemis Festival

Celebrated in Ladakh to honor Guru Padmasambhava with dance and music.

Tulip Festival

A vibrant spring festival in Srinagar showcasing tulip blooms.

Rogan Josh

Aromatic lamb curry with rich spices.

Kahwa

Traditional green tea infused with saffron and nuts.

Location

Jammu and Kashmir is in northern India, bordered by Pakistan, China, Ladakh, and Punjab.

Lidder River

The Lidder River flows through the Kashmir Valley, originating from the Kolahoi Glacier and merging with the Jhelum River.
